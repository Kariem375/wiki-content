# 📦 Item Bank & Auto-Sell Guide

> **Requirement:** You must have the **Item Bank perk** unlocked before this feature is available.

---

## What Is It?
The Item Bank combined with Auto-Sell lets you **passively sell resources on the Stock Market automatically** — even while you're offline. Perfect for making passive RP income without having to manually list items.

---

## Setup Guide

### Step 1 — Open the Bank
Type `/s` in chat → go to **Stock Market** tab → click **BANK** in the left sidebar.

![Bank tab in Stock Market](assets/auto_sell/bank-tab.png)

---

### Step 2 — Deposit Your Items
Add the items you want to auto-sell into the bank. These act as your stock supply — once they sell out, auto-selling stops until you refill.

---

### Step 3 — Find Your Item on the Market
Navigate back to the **Stock Market** and search for the item you just deposited.

---

### Step 4 — Set Your Auto-Sell Price
Click the item to open **Stock Item Details** → find the **AUTO SELL** price field → enter your desired price per unit and confirm.

![Auto-sell price field](assets/auto_sell/auto-sell.png)

---

### Step 5 — Confirmation
You'll see a popup confirming it worked:

![Confirmation popup](assets/auto_sell/confirmation.png)

---

## 💡 Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| 🔄 Refill regularly | Once your bank stock runs out, auto-selling stops |
| 📊 Check price history | Use the chart on the item details page to price competitively |
| ⭐ Use Favourites | Star frequently sold items for quicker access |

---

## Summary Flow

```
Unlock Perk → Deposit to Bank → Find Item on Market → Set Auto-Sell Price → Profit → Refill
```
